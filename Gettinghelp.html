Getting Help

This Help file can be launched From the Tray or from the Hotlist help menu. Pressing F1 key while in any of the Gui window will open help. Pressing F1 key while various controls have the focus may show help specific the that control or window.


For help on AutoHotkey it is recommended to visit the AutoHotkey Forms and go through the AutoHotkey Documentation.
